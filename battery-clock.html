Battery Clock

In my spare time, I've been learning a new skill... developing for Android. I haven't made a mobile app for over 10 years and that was on iOS. In the meantime I've mostly been doing C# corporate stuff, so this seemed like a fun diversion.

Currently I have submitted the app to Google, and hope it gets the OK for release (can't see why not, it's useful, customisable and does no shady data collection type things.)

Until it's released, you can view my empty store front here.

I'm hoping to charge a small fee, £0.50 for my hard work (believe me, some of it had me pulling my hair out), but these days I think people expect all apps to be free, so I'll see how it goes. If no-one buys it, I'll make it free after a while.
